WHO I AM

I am a Systems Engineer with a concentration in data analytics at George Mason University. The main reason I wanted to create this blog is to showcase my different interests that vary from time to time. "Jack of all trades, master of none"? Maybe, but I am a firm believer that you should try the things that interest you at an early age to figure out what you like before you get older and take on more responsibilities. My interests that I try to do on a daily basis includes(but not limited to): videography, photography, music, soccer, learning to play the piano, working out, and kicking it back with friends.

"When you learn, teach. When you get, give."
-Maya Angelou

I love to be around people. I love learning about everyone's story. I love the meaningful connection that can be formed with others. I believe that social media is a wonderful platform that allows us to stay connected like never before. We can be thousands of miles away from each other but still see each others faces. How incredible is that?!

"Someone's sitting in the shade today because someone planted a tree a long time ago."
-Warren Buffett

I've learned that it's ok to take risks, it's ok to mess up, and it's ok to do the opposite of everyone else is doing. I have spent so much time trying to fit in with everyone else that now I am trying to fit out. I would say that some of my ambitions and goals aren't what every 21 year old wants and I am completely fine with that because it is about what you want, not your family or friends. Although, I am thankful to have family and friends that support my goals.

I am currently 21 years old and these are my thoughts on "Who I Am", and obviously things can change because there is still a lot left to live for. I wanted to create this blog to inspire like-minded individuals and hopefully put a smile on at least one person's face every day.
